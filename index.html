Locations

Altoona Railroaders Memorial Museum

The only interactive railroaders museum in the United States. Film, museum, lobby show & gift shop.

Allegheny Portage Railroad National Historic Site

See historic railroad remains, the Lemon House and the Staple Bend tunnel.

Horseshoe Curve

One of the world's great engineering feats. Ride the funicular or walk 194 landscaped steps up to the train tracks.

Baker Mansion

Home of legendary Ironmaster Elias Baker and his family. Now the residence of the Blair County Historical Society.

Fort Roberdeau Historic Site

A 1778 Colonial Fort residing in Blair County. Tours, exhibits and nature trails. Innovative educational program.

Gallitzen Tunnels Park & Museum

See locomotives emerge from the Allegheny Tunnel and visit a 1942 PRR Caboose.
